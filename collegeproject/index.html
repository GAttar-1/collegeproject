Welcome to the Collegiate Archery Society

We are the Collegiate Archery Society, a group focused on college students who have an interest in archery. We are chapter-based and have many local organizations and dozens of universities. Our clubs have meetings that involve discussing the sport, signing up and organizing tournaments (within the club and competitive ones), discussing the history of archery, teaching technical skills, and of course, practicing! We are committed to keeping this ancient sport alive and active in a fun and exciting way. We hope to see you at one of our clubs, and if there is not one at your school, consider starting a chapter with us!
